Our Manufacturing Standard

We follow a rigorous 6-step industrial process to ensure every cup is leak-proof, food-safe, and export-quality.

1. Raw Material Sourcing

We source premium food-grade paperboard coated with PE (Polyethylene) to ensure water resistance and safety.

1

2. Precision Printing

Using Flexographic printing, we apply your brand logo and designs with food-safe ink before the paper is cut.

2

3. Die Cutting

The printed rolls are punched into "fan" shapes (sidewalls) and circular bottoms with extreme precision.

3

4. High-Speed Forming

Our machines fold the fans into cylinders and heat-seal the bottoms to prevent any leakage.

4

5. Quality Control

Every batch undergoes leak testing and visual inspection to meet international export standards.
